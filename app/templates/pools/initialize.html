What happens when you initialize:
  • A closed snapshot is created for the cutoff month with a $1.00 unit price
  • Each fund receives units proportional to its value (corpus + earnings)
  • All existing contributions are stamped as bought-in at $1.00
  • From the next month forward, normal month-close will allocate earnings by unit ownership
