How earnings are calculated:

Each month, the pool's total value (from vehicle ending balances) is adjusted by subtracting new contribution cash and adding back distribution cash. The adjusted value is divided by existing units to get the unit price. Each fund's value = units held × unit price. Earnings for the month = change in fund value − new contributions + distributions.
